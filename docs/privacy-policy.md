# Privacy Policy

**White Stone**
*Last updated: 15 February 2026*

## Overview

White Stone is a mental tracking app that helps spiritual practitioners track the goodness of their thoughts and actions throughout a day. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection

White Stone does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

## What We Don't Do

- We do not collect analytics or usage data
- We do not use third-party tracking or advertising SDKs
- We do not require an account or login
- We do not access your contacts, location, camera, microphone, or any other device sensors
- We do not share any data with third parties

## Local Data

The only data White Stone stores is the stones you log (type, timestamp, and optional notes). This data lives entirely on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed from your device.

## Children's Privacy

White Stone does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app does not collect information from any user.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated date.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/fatmonky/white-stone-app).
